Completing the Sale

Part of providing the best customer experience is presenting each customer with the customized options which enhance their recent purchase.

Logic Menu provides a customer centric, no pressure, approach to ensure each customer is given the opportunity to enhance their purchase.

Customer Specific

Before finalizing the sale, the Finance Manager adds the appropriate product selections to the customers profile. The customer can receive the custom link through either an SMS message or email. Optionally the customer can complete on a dealer computer or tablet.

Ease to Use

An intuitive and customer focused interface provides an easy to use experience with no explanation necessary. Once the customer receives their custom link, they are educated on each product before making a selection wether to accept or decline the recommendation.

Each interface is designed to work on an any device including phone, tablet, laptop or desktop computer.
